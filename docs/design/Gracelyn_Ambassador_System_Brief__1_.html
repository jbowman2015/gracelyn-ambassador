Ambassador engagement tracks
Standard All ambassadors at activation. Weekly email following the four-week rotating calendar (Mission Moment, Success Story by role category, Ambassador Spotlight, Program Update). Full referral ask cadence. Returns here from Alternative on first referral.
Alternative Active 90+ days with zero referrals. Bi-weekly email. Three content types rotating monthly: story submission invitation, experience sharing invitation, one-tap referral ask (one person, one message, no pressure). Automatic return to Standard on first referral.
Dormant 30+ days no email engagement and no referral activity. Day 30: re-engagement attempt 1. Day 45: attempt 2 with different framing. Day 60: escalated to coordinator for human outreach decision. No automated contact after Day 60 until coordinator re-activates.

Build timeline
Weeks 1–2 — Parmeet pre-build
57 Ambassador module fields, 3 new CRM modules, 9 WorkDrive folders, 8 Zoho Forms, 10 Zoho Flows, Wise and Tremendous accounts. Agent 5A validates all environment variables before any developer writes production code.
Weeks 3–7 — Parallel build (11 developers)
Each developer builds against their Agent Project Document v2.0. Weekly architect sync. Three cross-agent coordination points confirmed at kickoff: Agent 3 and 6 on ROLE_CATEGORY header format; Agent 4 and 5 on Support Tickets SLA field names; Agent 4 and 5 on escalation timestamp identity.
Weeks 7–9 — Integration sprint
End-to-end pipeline test. VIP Prospect Pipeline validated. International payment routing confirmed for all four methods. Support escalation ten-field webhook tested. SLA tracking and breach detection confirmed.
Weeks 9–11 — Compliance and engagement tuning
Phase 1 manual approvals live. Weekly engagement cycle stress-tested at scale with batch concurrency. Content calendar populated across all four role categories. Combined compliance form tested end-to-end.
Weeks 11–14 — Launch and scale
First ambassador cohort activated. VIP Prospect Pipeline cultivation begins. Auto-approve threshold monitoring active. Story buffer monitoring confirmed. System declared production-ready.
